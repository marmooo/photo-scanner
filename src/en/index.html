Photo Scanner

An app that streamlines document scanning on smartphones and PCs. The photo is automatically corrected like a scanner image. Of course, you can also manually adjust perspective projection, color correction, and noise removal.

First, specify the image you want to scan. Specifications from the clipboard, file selection, and camera capture are supported.
